Data Deletion Instructions

Last Updated: December 3, 2025

⚠️ Important: Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data and cannot be undone. Please ensure you have backed up any important information before proceeding.

1. Overview

At RoboCleanerBuddy, we respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your account and associated data from our system.

2. What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and profile picture
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials and authentication tokens
  • User Profile: All profile information and preferences
  • App Data: All data stored in your account within the app
  • Usage History: Activity logs and usage data

3. How to Request Data Deletion

Method 1: Through the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the RoboCleanerBuddy app
  2. Go to Settings or Account Settings
  3. Find the "Delete Account" or "Account Deletion" option
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions
  5. Confirm your deletion request
  6. Your account will be deleted within 30 days

Method 2: Via Email Request

  1. Send an email to: support@robocleanerbuddy.com
  2. Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the following information:
    • Your registered email address
    • Your full name (as registered)
    • Confirmation that you want to delete your account
  4. We will verify your identity and process your request
  5. You will receive a confirmation email within 48 hours
  6. Your account will be deleted within 30 days of verification

Method 3: Through Firebase Console (For Advanced Users)

If you signed in with Google or Facebook:

  1. Go to your Google Account settings or Facebook settings
  2. Find "Third-party apps" or "Apps and websites"
  3. Locate "RoboCleanerBuddy" in the list
  4. Click "Remove" or "Revoke Access"
  5. Contact us at support@robocleanerbuddy.com to complete the deletion

4. Processing Time

  • Immediate: Your account will be deactivated immediately upon request
  • Within 30 days: All data will be permanently deleted from our systems
  • Backup systems: Data may remain in backups for up to 90 days before final deletion

5. What Happens After Deletion?

  • You will no longer be able to sign in to the app
  • All your data will be permanently removed
  • You will not receive any further communications from us
  • You can create a new account at any time (if you wish)

6. Data Retention Exceptions

Some data may be retained for legal or operational purposes:

  • Legal Requirements: Data required by law or court order
  • Fraud Prevention: Information related to security incidents
  • Audit Logs: Anonymized logs for system security (no personal identifiers)

7. Third-Party Data

If you signed in using Google or Facebook, you may also need to:

  • Revoke app access in your Google Account settings
  • Remove the app from your Facebook connected apps
  • Contact those providers directly for their data deletion policies

8. Verification Process

To protect your account, we may need to verify your identity before processing the deletion request. This may include:

  • Email verification
  • Security questions
  • Additional identification if required

9. Canceling a Deletion Request

If you change your mind within 7 days of requesting deletion, you can cancel by:

  • Contacting us at support@robocleanerbuddy.com
  • Signing in to your account (if still active)
  • Following the cancellation instructions we provide

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about data deletion or need assistance:

Email: support@robocleanerbuddy.com
Subject: Data Deletion Request
Response Time: Within 48 hours

App Name: RoboCleanerBuddy
Developer: RoboCleanerBuddy Team

11. Your Rights

Under data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (as described on this page)
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request data portability

12. Additional Information

For more information about how we handle your data, please see our Privacy Policy.

⚠️ Final Warning: Once your account is deleted, this action cannot be reversed. All your data will be permanently removed. Please ensure you have backed up any information you wish to keep before proceeding with account deletion.
